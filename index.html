Welcome To

The Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce Foundation of Essex County

Giving Back To The City of Newark

The New Jersey Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce Foundation for Essex County was founded in 2000 by a group of community activists as a response to improving the living conditions of people across generations with an emphasis on youth.

HACCF is designed to provide social services and activities that respond to education, economic, social, cultural, health concerns and the needs of our community

The mission of The Foundation is to build relationships, provide guidance and coordinate programs to facilitate the success of our stakeholders.

Our Mission

The foundation is dedicated to helping individuals in financial need through a scholarship program for residents of Newark, NJ and neighboring cities, who are continuing their education.

Our goal is to help deserving individuals within our community to achieve their career goals, an objective which has the added benefit of improving the community at large.
